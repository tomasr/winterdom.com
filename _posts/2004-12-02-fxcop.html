I do have a problem with it, though, that perhaps someone can answer. Is there a way to disable the identifier spelling rules?

See, the thing is our code throws hundreds of errors with those rules, mostly because our code is written in spanish (and a mix of english on some parts, don't ask). I did see a way to check the dictionary language in the settings dialogs, but that's not what I want, I just want to ignore the rule altogether. Any clues?
