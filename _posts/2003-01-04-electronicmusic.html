Went through the local Tower Records store this afternoon, and after much ruffling around, found something I liked in the electronic music section. Paul Oakenfold's bunkka.

I'm very picky about electronic music, and overall, I didn't like Oakenfold's work much, but I really enjoyed some of the tracks on this one.
