You need to fix up your href's before including them within xhtml+body. Just click on your link above to the rss.xml file and you'll see IE's xml parser complain about missing semicolons. Of course what is happening is that an url like
http:||example.com/t.html?q1=123&q2=456 is invalid xml ...

& indicates the start of a html-entity and so the above has to be escaped out and coverted into
http:||example.com/t.html?q1=123&amp;q2=456
