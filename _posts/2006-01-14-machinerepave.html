Like Scott, I recently was paving my machine, but in my case it was getting my new laptop set up :).

My choice of basic tools for my machine is a little different than Scott's, though, so here's my list in case anyone is interested :)

  • Office
  • Firefox 1.5, with the Qute theme
  • Virtual PC 2004
  • VLC for video playback and watching DVDs
  • iTunes (for my iPod Shuffle)
  • Windows Anti-spyware beta
  • Console, I love having a console docked to my desktop for easy access (with Consolas as the font, of course)
  • CDBurner XP Pro 3.0
  • IsoBuster
  • SyncToy (great for quick backups and synchronizing drives and machines)
  • Newsgator, which still is my aggregator of choice
  • AVG
  • WindowClippings
  • FoxIt PDF Reader (amazing tool, thanks for the link Scott!)
  • ActivePerl
  • Unix Command Line tools for Win32

This time I'm trying to practice keeping all my development stuff on VPC images, so I created one for VS.NET 2003 + BizTalk 2004 development, another for just VS 2005, and another for BTS2006 + WinFx development.
