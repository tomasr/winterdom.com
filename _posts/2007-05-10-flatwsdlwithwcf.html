My good friend Christian Weyer shows how to take advantage of WCF's extensive extensibility model to improve interoperability with other Web Services stacks by simplifying the WSDL generated by WCF to get rid of all the and factored WSDL files. Here, he takes my InlineXsdInWsdl behavior to new heights and adds a bunch of hosting goodness to make using it a lot simpler.

Cool stuff, and really useful stuff to have around! Thanks for sharing!
