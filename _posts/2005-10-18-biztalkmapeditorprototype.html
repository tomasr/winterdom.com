Eddie Churchill is showing off prototypes of the BizTalk XSLT Map Editor for BizTalk 200X where X > 6. Very, very nice stuff. I particularly liked the way the feature of highlighting and bringing into view the selected link and link ends, though I'm not sure I liked the tick marks on the scrollbar as much :)

Together with the BizTalk Solution Designer, this is shaping up for a very exciting BizTalk future!
