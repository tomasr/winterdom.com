In the email Kevin Downs sent to the NDoc community (as posted by Charles C. Chen here), he mentioned that "In fact, were it not for Oleg Tkachenko’s kind donation of a MS MVP
MSDN subscription, I would not even have a copy of VS2005 to work with!". I think this is a sad state of affairs.

So what I'd like to do is extend an offer for a similar contribution to a leader or significant participant of an community project in the .NET and general Microsoft developer community, by giving away my last MVP Invite card for an MSDN Subscription!

So, what I'd like is for you, my dilligent reader, to nominate whoever you think most needs and deserves this. Just leave a comment on this post or send me an email. Be sure to mention why you think they deserve it the most :) I'll take it into consideration and later announce the winner on this blog!

Update (2006-08-09): Come'on people! I need more candidates! Keep'em coming!
