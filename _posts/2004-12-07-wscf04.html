Christian Weyer just announced the release of WSCF 0.4. This is very exciting for me, not only because of the new features it brings to the table (the WSDL wizard rocks!), but also because I was lucky enough to be allowed to try early copies of the tool, and immediately saw the great potential it has to improve how we approach our webservices creation process. I wholeheartedly recommend anyone working with ASMX WebServices to give it a spin.

Congrats my friend!
