I decided to download Collaxa's BPEL Server beta 9 trial today and see what it was about. As many others, I'm certainly interested enough in BEPL4WS (and anxious to see what ends up in the next release of Biztalk Server.

I've only been playing for a couple of hours with Collaxa's offering yet, but I'm already pretty impressed by what I've seen. The BPEL Console (showed in the image) is pretty nifty, and the debugging, tracing and auditing facilities seem like killer features. I'm looking forward to exploring what it offers in the next few days...
