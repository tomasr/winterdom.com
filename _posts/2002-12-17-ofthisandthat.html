Miscellaneous bits here and there. I started reading through Martin Fowler's new book yesterday, and imagine my surprise to find my name listed on the book's preface. Sure, I had written a couple of comments to the author back when he had his ISA pages up (nothing big, really), but I never got any kind of reply, so I assumed he never got it, or didn't read it. Guess, I was wrong :)
