Sam's musings on SciFi books made me think again about my all-time favorite SciFi books of all times. My personal favorites would be, after some hard choices, the entire Dune Saga (Frank Herbert) and Isaac Asimov's The End of Eternity.

Among shorter SciFi stories, one I particularly liked (although I'm still not completely shure why) is Brian Stableford's Mortimer Gray's History of Death, which was published in the 1996 "The year's best science fiction, thirteenth annual collection".
