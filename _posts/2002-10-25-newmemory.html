I just unpacked and installed an extra 256MB module on my Notebook (a Compaq Presario 2800), bringing it to 512MB. I got my memory from Crucial, which is a very nice site to buy memory from, as they have good prices and delivered fast!

I would've gone for a full 512MB module, but no way I could afford it. It's feeling much more responsive now, though, and they'll certainly come in handy while playing Dungeon Siege :)
