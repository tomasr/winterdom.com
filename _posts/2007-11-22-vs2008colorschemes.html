I've now added to each color scheme post a new download link to the 2008 version of the scheme. Here's the complete list:

  • Desert Nights
  • Ragnarok Blue
  • Ragnarok Grey
  • Nightingale
  • Garden of Eden
  • Moria Alternate
  • Dark Grey
