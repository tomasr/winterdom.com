While browsing around today I found out that one of the best computer-related magazines I've ever read is not longer being published. The magazine was Server/Workstation Expert (formerly known as SunExpert Magazine). It's a crying shame, really, cause it had, imho, wonderful writers!

So, if you feel like doing some reading, be sure to check the magazine's archives and read a few of Michael O'Brien's Ask Mr. Protocol columns, which are located on the C1 section of the archive.
