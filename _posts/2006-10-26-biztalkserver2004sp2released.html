Microsoft has released Service Pack 2 for BizTalk Server 2004 (found out via Tim Rayburn). You can find the download here, and the list of fixes included in SP2 can be found in KB article 924330. Many of the included fixes have been available for some time now as hotfixes, but it's far better to have them put together as a single supported package.
