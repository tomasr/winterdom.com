Jeremy Miller asks an informal poll of people using Open Source Tools for .NET Development. The answer is a resounding yes, I/we do. Here are some of the tools I'm using or used in the past:

  • NUnit
  • CVS
  • TotoiseCVS
  • Subversion
  • NAnt
  • RhinoMocks
  • The Castle Project (Windsor, in particular)
  • NHibernate
  • Log4Net
  • WinMerge
  • GVim
  • CruiseControl.NET
  • SharpDevelop
  • Wix
  • ... and more

Several of those I consider critical parts of my toolbox and wouldn't even dream of starting a project without them.
