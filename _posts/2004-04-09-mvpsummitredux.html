I spent all week in Seattle/Redmond at the Global MVP Summit. It was a blast.

On Saturday, got to finally meet my friend Sam Gentile Face to Face after all this time and had dinner with him and a few other guys.

On Monday, had a blast at Jillian's with the VC++ team and the rest of the VC++ MVPs

On Wednesday, I went to dinner alongside James Curran, Carl Daniel, Kim Gräsman and someone else whose name I can't quite remember now...(all VC++ MVPs). We looked around for a while, and ended up on this really nice place called The Pink Door; located close to Pike's Market. It had great ambient, great food, and the company was superb.
